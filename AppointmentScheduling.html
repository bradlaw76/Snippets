UX Design Notes

  • Progressive Disclosure: Steps are hidden until requirements (service/time) are met to reduce cognitive load.
  • Anonymized Scheduling: "Specialists" are referenced generally. No agent names or IDs are shown to maintain internal security.
  • Visual Cues: Government masthead and "Verified" status icons build immediate trust after identity handoff.
  • Neutral Palette: USWDS-inspired blue (#005ea2) denotes official status without overwhelming brand bias.
